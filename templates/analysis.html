About YouTube Comment Sentiment Analysis

YouTube Comment Sentiment Analysis is a project aimed at providing insights into the sentiment of comments on YouTube videos. By analyzing the language used in comments, we can understand the overall sentiment of viewers towards the video content.

How It Works

The project uses natural language processing (NLP) techniques to analyze the text of YouTube comments. First, the comments are collected using the YouTube Data API. Then, sentiment analysis algorithms are applied to determine whether the comments are positive, negative, or neutral.

The sentiment analysis results are then aggregated to provide an overall sentiment score for the video. This score can help content creators understand the reception of their videos and make informed decisions about their content strategy.
